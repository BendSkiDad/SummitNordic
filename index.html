Summit Nordic

The Summit High School Nordic team is a Nordic ski racing team made up of students from Summit High School in Bend, Oregon. We welcome student-athletes of all experience and ability levels. Our team values inclusivity and wants everyone interested in Nordic skiing to feel welcome and valued.

  • The Summit Nordic team is part of the Oregon Interscholastic Ski Racing Association Nordic (OISRAN).
  • The Summit High School Nordic Team is a based in Bend, Oregon.
  • The Summit Nordic team is affiliated with Summit High School.
  • The team practices each Saturday (usually in the morning) and Wednesday after school at Mt. Bachelor or Meissner Snow Park during the winter school sports season from mid November to near the end of February.
  • The head coach is JD Downing assisted by Gene Hyde, Lev Stryker, Jeff Doering, and Brian Harris. For more information please contact JD at jd@xcoregon.org.
  • The team uses the sportsYou app and website for communication.
